The Problem

Enterprise AI shouldn't be a forced tradeoff

Today you either give up control for speed, or keep control and spend months building plumbing instead of intelligence.

Option A: SaaS Chatbot Platforms

Fast to start. Impossible to own.

Vendor-hosted platforms get you running quickly — then lock you in.

  • Customer data leaves your infrastructure
  • Locked to one or two LLM providers
  • Per-conversation pricing explodes at scale
  • 3–6 month security review per new vendor
  • No visibility into model behavior
Option B: DIY with LLM APIs

Full control. Months of plumbing.

Building from scratch with LangChain or raw APIs gives you control, but at massive cost.

  • No dialogue management or state machine
  • Build your own observability from scratch
  • No governance, RBAC, or audit logging
  • Every channel requires a custom adapter
  • 60% of engineering time on undifferentiated work

Rasa: Enterprise AI you actually own

A self-hosted, LLM-agnostic orchestration platform purpose-built for conversational AI. Full control. Production-ready from day one.
